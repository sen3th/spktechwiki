# Allen & Heath ZED-428 (Large Analog Board)

<img src="/img/z428.png" width="75%" />

Analog sound board with 24 mic/instrument cable inputs.

## What we use it for?
Rare events that need a large number of inputs(more than the wireless board) or when other boards aren't functioning properly. Not commonly used.

## Where it is?
On the storage cubby on the corner.

## Basic usage
:::note
Mute buttons on th board may not function on the first try; Press them a couple of times until they work.
:::
- Carefully lift and put the board in a stable surface or just use it on the storage cubby.
- Plug in the board and power it on.
- Plug in a XLR cable from the board's main outputs to the caf sound board or a speaker.
- Plug in any microphones or instruments to the inputs to the board.
