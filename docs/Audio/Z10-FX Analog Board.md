# Allen & Heath ZED-10 (Analog Board)

<img src="/img/z10.png" width="75%" />

Analog sound board with 4 mic/instrument cable inputs and 2 stereo line inputs.

## What we use it for?
Any event that needs audio mixing but doesn't need a lot of inputs, like plays, small performances, pep rallies, etc that use only a few microphones, bluetooth inputs or instruments.

## Where it is?
On the projector cart or on the big sound board in the corner.

## Basic usage
- Plug in the board and power it on.
- Plug in a XLR cable from the board's main outputs to the caf sound board or a speaker.
- Plug in any microphones or instruments to the inputs to the board.
