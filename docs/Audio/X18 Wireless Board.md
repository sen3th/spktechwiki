# Behringer X18 Digital Mixer (Wireless Board)

<img src="/img/x18.png" width="75%" />


:::warning
Bus outputs for monitors aren't working as intended; Use another sound board if you wish to use monitors.
:::

## What this is
Digital sound board with 18 inputs, usually used for events with lots of inputs and lots of audio mixing needs. You need to use the iPad to control it wirelessly as it doesn't have physical controls. The Router is needed to connect to the board wirelessly.

## What we use it for
Moderately advanced audio mixing for events and performances like coffee houses, performances with a lot of inputs, etc.

## Where it is
On the projector cart or on the big sound board in the corner.

## Basic usage
- Plug in the board and power it on.
- Plug in the router and power it on.
- Connect the router to the ethernet port on the back of the board via the ethernet cable.
- On the iPad, connect to the wifi "Tech Team Only Wifi".
- Open the X Air app on the iPad.
- In the app, the board will appear once its connected to the wifi.
- Select the board and Voila!
- Plug in a XLR cable from the board's main outputs to the caf sound board or a speaker.
- Plug in any microphones or instruments to the inputs to the board.
