# ETC Colorsource 40 AV (Lighting Board)

<img src="/img/colorsource40av.png" width="75%" />

Lighting board with 40 channels and built-in AV control capabilities.

## What we use it for?
Any event that needs stage lighting control, like plays, performances, coffee houses, etc.

## Where it is?
On the big sound board in the corner.

## Basic usage
:::warning
The power cable for this board is very fragile and should be handled with care as it can easily be damaged.
:::
- Plug in the board with the power cable.
- Plug in a DMX cable from the board's DMX1 output on the back to the lighting port on the wall.
- Power on the board.
- Use the touchscreen and physical controls to control lighting scenes and effects.
